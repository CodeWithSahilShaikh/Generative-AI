**Vectors**: A mathematical list of numbers (coordinates) that pinpoint a location in a multi-dimensional space.

**Vector Embeddings**: A specialized vector that represents the meaning of a specific piece of data. For example, the word "happy" and the word "joyful" will have very similar vector embeddings because they mean similar things, even though their raw letters are completely different

Note: Embeddings is the process jisme ham words ko vector me and vector ko words me convert krte hai.

Note: Human brain toh 3 dmension tk cheezo ko visualizze kr pata hai but AI 1200, 3500+ dimensions bhi nikal skta hai.

**Vector Database** : Inn type ki embeddings ko store krne ke liye ghm vector database ka use krte hai.
Example: Pinecone.
